CV & Exhibitions

Zhe Ren is a multimedia artist, designer, and educator working across digital print, video, mixed reality, and virtual interaction. His work has been exhibited and screened internationally — including at the Getty Center, Harvard University, Musée Cernuschi (Paris), and the CICA Museum (South Korea) — and his research has been published in the United States, China, England, and South Korea.
